The Century Code

Look at the first two digits of the year:

1900s0

STEP 2
Year
The Year Number

Take the last two digits of the year.

1985 → 85
2012 → 12
2000 → 00

STEP 3
Leap Year
The Leap Year Number

Divide the Step 2 number by 4. Keep only the whole number — drop the remainder.

85 ÷ 4 = 21.25 → 21
If Step 2 is less than 4, the answer is 0

STEP 4
Date
The Day of the Month

Simply use the date number itself.

15th → 15
3rd → 3
31st → 31
